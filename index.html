Bachelor in Applied Computing Technology

The BACT is a four year degree program that focuses on the practical Aspects of the sense of computing. The develops core skills in multimedia technology, web development and network/system administration. Furthermore, it prepares learners supervisory and management skills. The curiculum is derived fromt be BS Industrial Technology major in Computer Technology with CHED Certificate of Program Compliance (COPC) No. 31, series of 21.


Program Objectives:

The program prepares learners with the practical skills in multimedia technology,
web/mobile development, and network systems administration,
and supervisorial skills to manage an organization in these ares of comptencies,
engage in a startup business of purpose teaching career.

Specific Objectives:

The learner should be able to:

1. Demonstrate understanding of the importance of being an ethical computing professional when making work-related.
2. Develop products or processes by applying their knowledge in computer network system, and development of productivity tools.
3. Participate as an active member of a development service team with leadership roles when appropriate.
4. Apply communication skills effectively to promote ideas, goals, processes or products.
5. Make positive contributions to the community with the skills and abilities learned in the program.
6. Pursue lifelong learning for personal and professional growth through varied experimental and fiel-based opportunities.

Job opportunities

  • Visual graphics designer
  • Photographer
  • Front-end web developer
  • Full-stack web developer
  • Systems/network administration
  • System maintenance personnel
  • Teacher/trainer
